Privacy Policy for AI Upload Booster

Last Updated: April 10, 2026

Simple Summary: AI Upload Booster does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All file processing happens locally in your browser. We don't have servers, we don't track you, and we can't see your files.

1. Data Collection

AI Upload Booster is designed with privacy as a core principle. To comply with Google Web Store policies, we explicitly state that our extension:

  • Does NOT collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as names or emails.
  • Does NOT collect or access the contents of the images, PDFs, or documents you process.
  • Does NOT track your browsing history, usage analytics, or telemetry.

2. Data Handling and Processing

How your data is handled when using the extension:

  • Local Processing Only: When you select images, PDFs, or Word documents, all merging and processing logic is executed locally using JavaScript within your browser environment.
  • No External Transmission: Your files are never transmitted to our servers, nor are they sent to any third-party processing servers by our extension.
  • AI Platform Injection: The merged file is prepared locally and injected directly into the active AI chat platform you are currently using.

3. Data Storage

Our data storage practices are strictly local:

  • No Server Storage: Because files never leave your device, we do not store your data on any cloud servers or databases.
  • Temporary Memory: Processed files are held only in your browser's temporary memory (RAM) and are cleared as soon as the task is completed or the tab is closed.
  • Local UI Preferences: The extension stores only one piece of non-sensitive data locally on your device: a simple true/false value indicating whether the floating widget button should be displayed. This contains no personal information.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

In strict accordance with user data privacy requirements:

  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not share, sell, or disclose any user data with any third parties, advertisers, or analytics providers.
  • No External Parties: There are zero external parties involved in the operation or processing of your files through this extension. What you upload stays entirely between your local device and the specific AI platform you choose to interact with.

5. Permissions Explanation

The extension requests the following permissions solely for its core functionality:

  • activeTab: To detect which AI platform you're currently using and inject the merged files.
  • storage: To remember your widget visibility preference locally.
  • scripting: To interact with the AI platform's file upload interface.
  • Host permissions: Limited strictly to specific AI chat platforms (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, DeepSeek, Mistral, Copilot, Grok) where the extension is designed to function.

6. Third-Party Services

AI Upload Booster does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The extension operates entirely offline within your browser.

7. Data Security

Since no data is collected or transmitted:

  • There are no servers that could be compromised.
  • There are no databases storing user information.
  • Your files remain completely private and under your control.

8. Children's Privacy

The extension does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13. Since no data collection occurs, the extension is safe for all age groups.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Material changes will be communicated through the Chrome Web Store listing.

10. Open Source Transparency

AI Upload Booster's source code is available for review. You can verify our privacy claims by examining the code yourself.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or the extension's data practices, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store support page.

12. Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there is no data to:

  • Request access to
  • Request deletion of
  • Request correction of
  • Request portability of

You maintain complete control over any files you process with the extension, as they never leave your device.

Your Privacy Guarantee: AI Upload Booster is a client-side tool. We have no backend infrastructure, no servers, and no ability to see or access your files. What you upload stays between you and the AI platform you choose to use.
